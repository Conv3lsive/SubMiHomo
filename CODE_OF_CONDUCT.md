# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. SubMiHomo is a collaborative project built by volunteers, and we believe that a respectful, inclusive environment benefits everyone.

We are dedicated to:

- **Being respectful**: We welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences
- **Being inclusive**: We actively work to make SubMiHomo accessible and welcoming to everyone
- **Being professional**: We communicate clearly and constructively, even in disagreement
- **Being safe**: We maintain a harassment-free environment for all participants

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate misgendering or use of "dead" or rejected names
- Threats of violence or incitement to violence
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Sustained disruption of community spaces (online or in-person)
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All SubMiHomo community spaces (GitHub, discussions, issue trackers, pull requests)
- Interactions between community members related to the project
- Public events or conferences where SubMiHomo is discussed

Moderators are expected to enforce this code fairly and consistently.

## Enforcement

Community members who witness or experience unacceptable behavior are encouraged to report it to the project maintainers. All reports will be handled confidentially.

Reports should be directed to:

📧 **[submihomo-conduct@example.com]** or a project maintainer via private message

**All reports will be:**

- Acknowledged within 48 hours
- Investigated thoroughly
- Treated with confidentiality (details shared only with those who need to know)
- Addressed according to the severity and context

**Enforcement steps may include:**

1. Private conversation with the person(s) involved
2. Public warning or removal of offensive content
3. Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
4. Escalation to hosting platforms (GitHub, etc.) if necessary
